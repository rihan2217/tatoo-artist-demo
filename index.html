The Artist

DEMO
SHARMA

Born into a family of artists, DEMO discovered tattooing at 19 — not as a trade, but as a calling. What began in a basement studio in South Delhi has evolved into a decade-long obsession with the intersection of pain, permanence, and art.

Trained under masters in Tokyo and Berlin, DEMO's work is impossible to categorize. Blackwork bleeds into realism. Fine lines erupt into texture. Every piece carries his signature tension between control and chaos — the feeling that something alive is trapped beneath the skin.

He doesn't take flash requests. He doesn't rush. If you want something real, something that will still hold meaning in thirty years — that's what he's here for.

Book a Session

MAKE
YOUR
MARK

Every booking starts with an honest conversation. Fill out the form — be as specific or as vague as you like. I'll reply within 48 hours with questions, thoughts, or a consultation time.

01

Deposit required. A non-refundable deposit secures your slot. This is applied to the final cost of your tattoo.

02

Design is shared 48h before. You'll see your design before the session — not on the day. Changes after this point may incur a fee.

03

Arrive sober, eat before. No alcohol 24h prior. Eat a proper meal. Wear clothing that gives easy access to the placement area.

04

No walk-ins. Every session is by appointment. This isn't a flash shop — it's a studio focused on intentional work.
